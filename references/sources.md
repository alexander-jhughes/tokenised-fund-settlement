## The Holy Grail of Cross-Border Payments
- Source: ECB Working Paper Series
- Link: https://www.ecb.europa.eu/pub/pdf/scpwps/ecb.wp2693~8d4e580438.en.pdf
- Key point: Stablecoins are among the most promising solutions for cross-border payments due to potential straight-through processing gains, but their adoption depends on resolving compliance (AML/CFT), financial stability, and monetary sovereignty concerns.
- Relevant to: problem statement (cost/friction of current cross-border settlement)

## Project Guardian: Operationalising Tokenised Funds
- Source: Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS), Project Guardian
- Link: https://www.mas.gov.sg/-/media/mas-media-library/development/fintech/guardian/guardian-funds-framework.pdf
- Key point: The Guardian Funds Framework defines three tokenised fund register models (Digital Mirror, Digital Twin, Digital Native), each requiring different technical and legal standards to operate.
- Relevant to: architecture.md (settlement/compliance model design)
